# Module 4: Supply Chain and Profitability Analysis

### Business Objective
To evaluate shipping efficiency across global distribution centers and cross-reference delivery windows with real product profit margins, pinpointing logistical bottlenecks that impact net revenue.

### Key Business Questions (Google Framework)
1. Which distribution centers experience the longest delays between order creation and shipping?
2. How does the shipping speed of each warehouse correlate with its total generated net profit?
3. What is the total volume of orders processed successfully by each logistics node?

### Tech Stack
* **Database:** Google BigQuery (Public Dataset: `thelook_ecommerce`)
* **Language:** SQL (Advanced Multi-table `JOINs`, Subqueries using CTEs, Aggregated Metrics, and Date Functions)

### Key Insights & Business Impact
* The query cross-references fulfillment times with product-level cost structures to deliver operational visibility to supply chain directors.
