# Module 3: Web Conversion Funnel Analysis

### Business Objective
To identify where potential customers drop off in the digital purchase funnel, enabling the UX and marketing teams to optimize the checkout process and recover lost revenue.

### Key Business Questions (Google Framework)
1. What percentage of users who view a product successfully add it to their shopping cart?
2. What is our cart abandonment (churn) rate during the final checkout stage?
3. What is the platform's baseline end-to-end conversion rate?

### Tech Stack
* **Database:** Google BigQuery (Public Dataset: `thelook_ecommerce`)
* **Language:** SQL (Advanced CTEs, Conditional Aggregations, and Mathematical Formatting)

### Key Insights & Business Impact
* The query executes correctly, analyzing the last 365 days of live digital event logs to pinpoint exact pipeline bottlenecks.
